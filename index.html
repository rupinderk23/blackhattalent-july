Shankar Ehsaan Loy
The iconic Musical Trio who has given evergreen hits
Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy’s journey toward becoming the revered musical Trio of Bollywood - began through their work in jingles and ad compositions. Ehsaan Noorani, the guitarist, and Loy Mendosa, the pianist, first met in 1989 while working for ad film composer Ranjit Barot, and their professional chemistry was undeniable. Their paths converged with Shankar Mahadevan when he was brought in by Ranjit Barot to sing for a Pepsi ad. The synergy between the three was palpable, leading them to collaborate on numerous ad jingles as a Trio.
And it was the music album of ‘Dil Chahta Hai’ (2001), ironically, a movie about friendship and love, that catapulted them to fame.
With a career spanning over two decades, Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy have revolutionized Hindi film music, composing over 50 soundtracks across multiple languages, winning numerous awards, and leaving an indelible mark as Bollywood's most influential and enduring music composers.

Vishal & Sheykhar
The Musical Duo who has composed some of the most iconic Bollywood songs
Two childhood friends who met in the studio while recording songs for a film decided to form a team together to write music for Bollywood films. And there they were, the iconic musical duo of Bollywood; Vishal & Sheykhar whose friendship culminated into a beautiful musical synergy belting out some of the evergreen chart-topping hits. From the revered music of ‘Jhankaar Beats’, and chart-topping albums of ‘Dus’, ‘Salaam Namaste’, ‘Om Shanti Om’, ‘Bachna Ae Haseeno’, ‘Dostana’, Vishal-Shekhar have positioned themselves as the stalwarts of the music industry. In the ever-evolving world of Bollywood music, Vishal & Sheykhar continue to enchant and enthrall listeners around the globe.

Abhinav Bindra
